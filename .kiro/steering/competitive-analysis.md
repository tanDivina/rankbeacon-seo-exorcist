This steering document guides competitive SEO analysis and threat assessment for the RankBeacon platform.

## Monster Classification System

### üëπ Monster Types (Competitor Threats)

**Alpha Monsters** - Direct competitors dominating your primary keywords
- Ranking in top 3 positions for your target keywords
- Strong domain authority (DA 60+)
- Comprehensive content coverage
- **Action**: Deep analysis required, long-term strategy needed

**Beta Monsters** - Emerging threats gaining ground
- Ranking positions 4-10 for target keywords

- Ranking for related/semantic keywords

- Featured in AI-generated summaries
- Strong for voice search queries
- High featured snippet capture rate
- **Action**: Optimize for AI visibility

## Competitive Response Strategies

### When Competitors Launch New Content

1. **Analyze the content**: Depth, format, keywords targeted
2. **Assess the threat**: Ranking potential, keyword overlap
3. **Determine response**:
   - Create superior content (10x better)
   - Target related long-tail keywords
   - Differentiate with unique angle or format
   - Ignore if low threat/relevance

### When Competitors Gain Rankings

1. **Identify the cause**: Algorithm update, new content, backlinks
2. **Analyze their improvements**: What changed technically or content-wise
3. **Develop counter-strategy**:
   - Improve your content quality
   - Build relevant backlinks
   - Optimize technical SEO
   - Target alternative keywords

### When Algorithm Updates Hit

1. **Monitor competitor impact**: Who gained, who lost
2. **Identify patterns**: What characteristics correlate with gains
3. **Adapt strategy**: Align with algorithm preferences
4. **Document learnings**: Build institutional knowledge
